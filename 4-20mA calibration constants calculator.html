4-20mA calibration constants calculator

This calculator estimates the impedance and equivalent series inductance of rectangular cross section ferrite toroid cored inductors at HF.

Inputs:
  Input Result
Scenario 1 (B) (A)
Scenario 2 (D) (C)
 
Results:
meas_mult
meas_intercept

The calculator does not do a lot of error checking, if you enter nonsense, it will produce nonsense. NaN means not a number, check the input values.
